# Code of Conduct

We want Mithai to be a practical, respectful project for maintainers, users, and contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but focus on facts and impact.
- Keep technical disagreements about the work, not the person.
- Avoid harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or community.

To report conduct issues, email security@last9.io with relevant context.
